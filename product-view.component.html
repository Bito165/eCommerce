Additional Notes

Points to note when you make the switch to an Organic Shampoo -

1. Since our shampoos do not contain surfactants they have a natural medium bubbly lather.

2. Oil tends to cut the lather, hence if your hair is heavily oiled, the first wash will not result in much lather. Second wash will result in a much better lather as the oil has been washed off in the first wash.

3. Hard water inhibits lather and we recommend to use soft water for better lathering and final result.

4. It can take a few washes for your scalp to adjust when switching to an organic shampoo. In the beginning, it may feel like your hair is oilier and heavier than normal. After a few washes, however, your scalp will adjust and you will find that your hair's texture is more well-balanced with less oil at the roots and healthier, moist tips.

5. You may feel dryness and limp hair in the initial stages of you switching to an organic shampoo.
